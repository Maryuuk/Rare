    Meet the best professionals

    Pablo Frias

    Inknation Studio

    Pablo Frias is a master of colors! His colorful pieces stand out immediately and are really one one of a kind. Pablo works out of the renowned InkNation studio in NYC.

    Alejandro Romano

    Stefano's Tattoo Studio NYC

    Alejandro is an artist at Stefano's Tattoo Studio. He is great with black&grey tattoos and is extremely precise. He also isn't afraid to use some bright colors!

    Mattia Bretti

    Soho Ink

    Mattia has been in the tattoo business since 1999 and an artist his entire life. He is an American traditional artist who work consisting of bold outline, heavy shading and vibrant colors. Mattia truly enjoys any tattoo that challenges him to be a better artist and is fun to do. Mattia is on the current season of Ink Master.

    Darwin Enriquez

    Inknation Studio

    Darwin Enriquez entered the world of body art at 20 years of age in his hometown of Valencia, Venezuela, where he began work creating designs for a tattoo shop. During his stint at the tattoo shop, he developed his artistic talents and felt an instant attraction to the art-form of tattooing.

    Benjamin Flores

    Five Points Tattoo NYC

    Benjamin is an artist at Five Points Tattoo Studio in NY. He specializes in Traditional tattoos.

    Roshi Kameha

    Asylum Tattoo

    Roshi tattoos out of Asylum Studio in the Bronx. He is a multi-talented artist and some of his specialties include lettering and fine line tattoos.
